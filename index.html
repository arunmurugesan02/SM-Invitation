How It All Began

"Arugil vegu dhinam irundhirpom…
Ariyaamal sendriruppom…
Vizhi thulaithadhai vidhi koduthu naam…
Viral arugile adhai niruthalam…"

🏫

Same school… but different classrooms,
Same place… but different stories.

🤫

Oru pakkam silent crush…
Inoru pakkam total unawareness

🤝

Time passed… paths changed…
Oru naal, through a common friend, we finally met.

💕

What started as a simple friendship,
slowly turned into something meaningful…
Then again, life paused us for a while.

✨

But some stories don't end that easily.

❤️

Years later, we met again…
This time, with clarity, courage, and feelings that stayed.
She expressed her heart
He took his time…
And finally, a "YES" that mattered — on her birthday, 2023

😄

Since then — memories, small fights, lots of laughter,
and a journey that brought us here today…

Turning our long story into a lifetime journey 💫
